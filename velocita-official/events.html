FORMULA BHARAT 2019

Formula Bharat 2019 was a student design competition organized by Mobility Engineering Consortium Pvt. Ltd. and was held in the Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore from January 23rd to January 27th, 2019. This was the first formula student competition of Velocita Racing. In the pre-registration quiz, Velocita Racing was placed 21st among 100+ national teams and was assigned car number 26 for the combustion class.

Being a first time team, Velocita Racing received a good amount of constructive criticism from the event judges which enabled the team to work on the flaws of VRF1 and to develop a better engineered formula car for the next season. The results of the competition are as follows:

1. 27th in business plan

2. 29th in cost and manufacturing

3. 46th in engineering design

4. 35th overall in statics among 70 teams

5. Won the T-shirt design competition

6. Featured 2 photos in the FB2019 official calendar

FORMULA BHARAT 2020

The 2020 season of Formula Bharat will be organised by Curiosum Tech Pvt. Ltd. in the Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore from January 22nd to January 26th, 2020. FB2019 set the tone right for Velocita Racing broadening their goals and ideas behind the second project. This race season, a set of dynamic, passionate and focused engineering undergraduates of Velocita Racing worked hard and the squad was placed 16th among 97 teams contending for 45 spots in the pre-registration quiz.
